About The Project

Beneficiaries

Our project is for the farmers of the Philippines who continuously struggle with the economy and the climate of our country, with constant monsoons all year round, affecting their living.

Our Purpose

As a team, we aim to develop an automated greenhouse technology that will aid farmers in managing their produce by controlling their preferred environment, watering system, and by keeping a log management system.

Project Plan

  1. Research and Development: Develop and test smart greenhouse technology.
  2. Education and Training: Provide farmers with the knowledge and training to use the technology effectively.
  3. Implementation: Assist farmers in setting up and operating smart greenhouses.
  4. Monitoring and Support: Continuously monitor and offer support to ensure successful crop cultivation.
  5. Data Collection: Gather data on crop yields, resource usage and environmental impact.
  6. Expansion: Scale the project to reach more farmers and regions.
